Goku is one of my favourtie character in DragonBallZ. He is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball metaseries. Goku is a Saiyan originally sent to Earth as an infant. He constantly strives and trains to be the greatest warrior possible, which has kept the Earth and the universe safe from destruction many times.
